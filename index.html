IMDb integration

Instead of being yet another movie service that requires you to rate movies, we re-use your ratings from IMDb. How cool is that?

Top Movie data

We use the MovieTweetings dataset which adds new movies on a daily basis to keep the movie catalog as interesting and relevant as possible.

You are in control

Control your recommended movies by changing settings, setting filters and rating more movies. We offer the interaction that IMDb lacks.

How it works

Step 1: Rate movies on the IMDb.com website.

Step 2: Set ratings public at the IMDb profile page.

Step 3: Install the IMDbrain extension, and log in.

Step 4: View and control your movie recommendations!

How it looks

About

IMDbrain, pronounce as 'I am the brain', is a chrome extension that integrates your IMDb ratings to provide more customizable movie recommendations. If you have an IMDb account, your ratings will be imported and provided to the brain.

You can get movie recommendations and more interestingly, you can fine-tune specific settings of the recommendation process to improve the quality of your movie recommendation list. Note that this is a beta service which may be slow, crash or freeze at times.

IMDbrain Login

When you use the extension for the first time, you will not be logged in. To do so, login on the IMDb website or simply refresh any IMDb page where you are already logged in.

IMDbrain LOGIN detected

Alright, you have refreshed an IMDb page where you were logged in and now the IMDbrain extension has detected your IMDb username. Logging in is now as easy as clicking a button!

Note, that we do not have access to your password. All authentication is handled by the IMDb website.

Popular recommendations

Hooray, you are logged in and getting movie suggestions. But wait ... the brain does not know you yet. First you must have rated at least 5 movies (not series) on IMDb and wait until the brain has analyzed your profile. This shouldn't take more than 30 minutes. In the meantime the brain shows some popular movies to keep you busy.You can already play around with the genre filters if you want.

Don't forget to set your ratings as 'public' on your IMDb profile page or the brain won't be able to access your profile.

Movie recommendations

Yes! The brain has prepared a list of movies that you might like based on your previous ratings. You can keep scrolling for more movies, open the corresponding IMDb pages (add movies to your watchlist maybe?) or hide movies you don't want to see. Don't forget to rate more movies once in a while, and refresh your list. The brain gets smarter with every rating and may have some new movies for you.

You are in control

Don't like your recommendations? Check the settings! The brain is very smart, but you know yourself better than anyone. Try updating the settings and see if you can improve your results.

Genre filters

Genre filters, yes sir! Improve your recommendation list by dragging the genres to the 'exclude' or 'include' list. Do you hate all romantic movies? Or maybe you are looking for a great comedy. Set the genre filters and the brain takes care of the rest!
